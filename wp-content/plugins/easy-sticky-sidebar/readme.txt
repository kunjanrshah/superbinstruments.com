== How does it work? == 

It's easy, download the plugin, replace the image, text, call to action text and url with something you want to promote like a new blog post or a hot selling product. Once you scroll the page, the WordPress CTA Plugin will collapse so it does not take up too much space on your website. You can open the the easy sticky sidebar again by clicking on the button.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `easy-sticky-sidebar` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory or search for "Easy Sticky Sidebar" on WordPress.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. You will find 'Easy Sticky sidebar' menu in your WordPress admin panel.
4. Once you are on the Easy Sticky Sidebar admin panel, change the image, text, fonts, colors and url of the Easy Sticky Sidebar then click save.
5. Once the page reloads, you will see the preview on the right hand side.
6. When the Easy Sticky Sidebar is ready to go live, click "no" on "development mode".
7. Refresh your any page on your website to see the Easy Sticky Sidebar. When you scroll down the Easy Sticky Sidebar will collapse so that it does not take up too much room.
